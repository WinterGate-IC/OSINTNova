# 🔍 OSINTNova 2026 – Investigation Report

---

## Executive Summary

This investigation examines **OSINTNova** (`app.osintnova.com`), a commercial OSINT platform offering a broad range of intelligence services including People Intelligence, Phone Intelligence, Vehicle Intelligence, Discord Intelligence, and various social media scraping tools.

**Key Finding:** The platform aggregates and sells access to Personally Identifiable Information (PII) through its "PRO" tier, with no visible privacy policy, terms of service, or consent mechanisms. This raises significant legal and ethical concerns under multiple federal and state laws.

**Investigation Status:** 🔴 Active

**Risk Level:** 🔴 HIGH (Legal violations identified)

## Investigation Findings

### 1. Platform Legitimacy & Reputation

- **ScamAdviser** rates `osint.nova-saas.com` with a trust score of **66/100** – "medium to low risk" 
- The domain is **very young** and has a **low Tranco rank** (few visitors) 
- A user review noted *"mixed feelings about it given the cost per search/report and the trial run I did revealed some missing or mixed up information"* 
- **No formal reviews** found on Trustpilot or major review platforms for `app.osintnova.com`

**Assessment:** The platform is relatively new, with low visibility and mixed early reviews.

### 2. Data Handling & Privacy Concerns

**Red Flags:**

1. **No visible Privacy Policy or Terms of Service** on the main platform page
2. **No opt-out mechanisms** for individuals whose data is being sold
3. **No data source disclosure** – unclear where PII is sourced from
4. **PII aggregation** – the `WhoAmI` and `Oracle` services compile comprehensive personal profiles
5. **Discord Intelligence** – likely violates Discord's Terms of Service (ToS)

### 3. Legal Exposure

| Statute | Potential Violation | Severity |
|---------|---------------------|----------|
| **18 U.S.C. § 1039** | Selling confidential phone records | 🔴 Federal Crime |
| **CCPA/CPRA** | Sale of PII without consent or opt-out | 🔴 High |
| **CFAA (18 U.S.C. § 1030)** | Unauthorized scraping of social media platforms | 🔴 High |
| **FTC Act § 5** | Unfair or deceptive trade practices | 🔴 High |
| **California Civil Code § 1724** | Unlawful sale of data obtained via crime | 🔴 High |
| **Platform ToS** | Discord, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, Steam scraping violations | 🔴 High |

### 4. Associated User Activity

A user named **Goofisded** (guns.lol/goofisded, GitHub: Goofisded) was observed:

- Holding `root` and `sudo` roles in OSINTNova's Discord server
- Being defensive, dismissive, and hostile when questioned about the platform's legality
- Timing out an investigator who asked legitimate legal questions

**Assessment:** Likely staff or close affiliate. Defensive behavior indicates awareness of legal exposure.
